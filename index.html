    About Mark

    Mark SubbaRao is an experienced museum professional who specializes in developing innovative methods for delivering Astronomy and Space Science content to the public. He is working to help transition the planetarium community to the big data era. He has produced, written and directed fulldome planetarium shows, stereoscopic films and special format media. He has decades of experience as an astronomy researcher, specializing in cosmography, the mapping of the Universe.

    • Mark is the Director of the Adler Planetarium's Space Visualiation Laboratory
