    About Mark

    Mark SubbaRao is an experienced museum professional who specializes in developing innovative methods for delivering Astronomy and Space Science content to the public. He is a leader in the planetarium community, working to transition the community to the big data era. He has produced, written and directed fulldome planetarium shows, stereoscopic films and special format media which have received both critical and popular acclaim. His scientific visualizations have been widely viewed in film, television and print. He has decades of experience as an astronomy researcher, specializing in cosmography, the mapping of the Universe.

    • Mark is the Director of the Adler Planetarium's Space Visualiation Laboratory
    • Mark the chair of the International Planetarium Society's Science and Data Visualization Task Force
